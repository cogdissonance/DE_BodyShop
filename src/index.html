About Us


D&E Bodyshop is a family run auto body repair and paint shop.

We paint all types of cars and also have a seperate location for painting boats. We offer very competitive pricing as well as great service.
If you live in the Miami area and have a car that needs body repair, paint, or a boat that needs painting, click the contact link above and give us a call or fill out the form and we'll promptly get back to you.
